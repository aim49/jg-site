HOW DO I REGISTER FOR PRAYER LINE? add

Registration for prayer line is done every Saturday at Rajiv Ghandi Hall between 12pm and 1pm. Please bring your ID. REGISTRATION IS FREE.

HOW DO I SEE THE MAN OF GOD ONE ON ONE?add

To see the man of God ONE on ONE you need to first be on the prayer line and during service you go to the information desk to register. If you are an international guest please go to our CONTACT US page and fill up the visitor's form.
